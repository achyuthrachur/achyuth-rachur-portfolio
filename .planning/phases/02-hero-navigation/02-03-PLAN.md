
**Why these placeholder sections matter:**
- `id="about"` through `id="contact"` — required for Nav's IntersectionObserver (`document.getElementById(id)`) to find them. Without these, the null guard fires and no section ever becomes active.
- `scroll-mt-16` — 64px scroll offset matching Nav's `h-16`. Without this, clicking a nav link positions the section title behind the sticky nav.
- `min-h-screen` — ensures each placeholder is tall enough for IntersectionObserver threshold=0.5 to fire when scrolled into view.
- Section background colors match the Phase 3/4 spec for continuity: about=bg-page (#f8f9fc), experience=bg-section (#f6f7fa), skills=bg-section-warm (#f0f2f8), education=bg-page, contact=bg-[#011E41]
